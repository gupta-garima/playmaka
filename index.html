The Difference

Why Sports Performance?

Athletic success is about more than talent alone. The athletes who reach their potential are those with access to quality coaching, structured training, and consistent guidance throughout their journey.



Start Training
01 — Sport-Specific
Training That Transfers

Sports performance goes beyond getting stronger. We develop strength, power, speed, agility, mobility, and injury resilience — qualities that directly translate to competition.

02 — Individualized
Built Around You

Every athlete has unique demands based on their sport, position, age, experience level, and competitive season. Your training reflects those needs — not a generic template.

03 — Accessible
No Matter Where You Are

We believe every athlete deserves access to high-quality performance coaching regardless of where they live, what resources their school has, or their level of competition.

04 — Long-Term
Habits That Last

A properly designed program prepares athletes for competition today and builds habits that support long-term athletic success well beyond their playing days.

About Spencer

Coached at Every Level.

My name is Spencer Shipman, founder of Playmaka Sports Performance. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Kinesiology and am a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS).

As an athlete, I competed at the junior college, NCAA Division III, and NCAA Division I levels — giving me firsthand experience with the physical and mental demands required at each stage. I've seen what access to quality coaching can do. I've also seen what happens when athletes don't have it. Playmaka was built to change that.

Our Approach

Three Principles.
One Standard.

01
Structure

Athletes perform best when training follows a long-term plan aligned with their sport, goals, and competitive calendar — not random workouts stitched together.

02
Consistency

Results are earned through repeated effort over time. Sustainable athletic development requires accountability and consistent execution, week after week.

03
Communication

Strong relationships between athletes, coaches, parents, and performance staff create better outcomes and stronger athletic cultures at every level.
